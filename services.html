Tree Removal

Safe, efficient removal of trees that pose a risk to your property, are dead or dying, or simply need to go.

When You Need Tree Removal:

  • Dead or dying trees that could fall
  • Trees too close to your home or structures
  • Storm-damaged trees creating hazards
  • Trees interfering with power lines
  • Clearing land for construction or landscaping
  • Diseased trees that could spread to healthy trees

Our Process:

We use advanced rigging techniques and professional-grade equipment to safely remove trees of any size. Every job includes:

  • Thorough site assessment and safety planning
  • Protection of surrounding property and landscaping
  • Piece-by-piece dismantling for tight spaces
  • Complete cleanup and debris removal
  • Optional stump grinding

Tree removal pricing varies by size and complexity.

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Expert pruning to keep your trees healthy, beautiful, and safe. Regular maintenance prevents damage and extends tree life.

Benefits of Professional Trimming:

  • Removes dead or diseased branches before they fall
  • Improves tree health and appearance
  • Increases sunlight to your yard and garden
  • Prevents branches from damaging your roof or siding
  • Reduces risk during storms
  • Enhances property value and curb appeal

Who Needs This Service:

Homeowners who want to maintain healthy trees, prevent storm damage, or improve their property's appearance. We recommend trimming every 3-5 years depending on tree species and growth rate.

What We Trim:

  • Overgrown branches near structures
  • Dead, damaged, or diseased limbs
  • Branches touching power lines (with utility clearance)
  • Crown thinning for better airflow
  • Canopy lifting for clearance

Keep your trees healthy and your property safe.

Stump Grinding & Removal

Complete stump removal to reclaim your yard space and prevent regrowth, pests, and safety hazards.

Why Remove Stumps:

  • Trip hazards for children and visitors
  • Attracts termites and other pests
  • Takes up valuable yard space
  • Makes mowing and landscaping difficult
  • Can sprout new growth
  • Reduces property appeal

Our Stump Grinding Process:

We use professional stump grinders to completely remove stumps below ground level. The process is fast, effective, and minimizes damage to your lawn.

  • Grind stump 6-12 inches below ground level
  • Remove wood chips or leave as mulch
  • Fill hole with topsoil if requested
  • Minimal lawn disruption
  • Same-day completion for most stumps

Stump grinding typically costs $100-$400 per stump.
