Common Questions

How quickly can you provide an estimate?

Most estimates are provided within 24 hours. For simpler projects, we can often give you a ballpark figure over the phone and schedule a detailed on-site assessment at your convenience.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, all estimates are completely free with no obligation. We'll assess your property, explain what needs to be done, and provide clear pricing before any work begins.

What information helps you give an accurate quote?

Photos are helpful if you have them. Tell us about tree size (height and trunk diameter if possible), location on your property, proximity to structures or power lines, and what you want done. Don't worry if you're not sure ‚Äî we'll figure it out together.

How far in advance should I schedule?

For routine work, we typically book 1-2 weeks out, though sometimes sooner. Emergency services are available 24/7 and we prioritize hazardous situations.
